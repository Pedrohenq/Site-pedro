# **App Name**: Belo Alimentos Corporativos

## Core Features:

- Company Presentation: Professionally showcase the company, highlighting its values, mission, and team.
- Service Showcase: Clearly present the range of food supply services for companies, including prepared foods, beverages, and various food products.
- Client Contact Form: Enable potential corporate clients to easily contact the company through a contact form, capturing necessary information for follow-up.
- Legal Information Display: Display essential legal information such as CNPJ and address to build trust and credibility.
- AI-Powered Menu Suggestion Tool: Suggest suitable meal options based on the dietary preferences of their user (their corporate client). An LLM is used as a tool.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant orange (#E67E22), representing the food focus of the company.
- Background color: Very light orange (#F8F3EC) with a desaturated look.
- Accent color: Teal (#2D9BD4), for a contrast that inspires trust.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a serif with an elegant feel.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a sans-serif, is paired with 'Playfair' for readability.
- Use clean, modern icons to represent the services offered, ensuring they align with the overall corporate aesthetic.
- Maintain a clean and well-structured layout with generous spacing and clear visual hierarchy, optimized for both desktop and mobile devices.
